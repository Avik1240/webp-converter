When SVG needs a WebP export

SVG is usually best for responsive icons and simple illustrations. Convert it to WebP when you need a fixed bitmap asset, such as for platforms that do not accept SVG uploads.

SVG to WebP FAQ

Should I always convert SVG to WebP?

No. Retain SVG for scalable vector graphics where the destination supports it; use WebP when a bitmap is required.

Will external SVG resources load?

For privacy and security, use self-contained SVG files without external images, fonts, or scripts.
