PNG to WebP FAQ

Does WebP preserve PNG transparency?

Yes. WebP supports alpha transparency, so transparent areas are retained during conversion.

Is WebP good for logos?

It can be. Test your logo at the intended quality level; SVG remains preferable for simple scalable vector artwork.
