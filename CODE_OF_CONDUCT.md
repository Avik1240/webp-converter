# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We are committed to a harassment-free, welcoming community. Be respectful, assume good intent, and focus discussion on ideas and evidence. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

Report concerns privately to the maintainers listed in the repository settings. Maintainers may clarify expectations, warn, remove content, or ban participants as appropriate. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.
