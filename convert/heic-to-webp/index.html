Why convert HEIC to WebP?

HEIC compresses well but causes compatibility headaches outside Apple's ecosystem. WebP is supported everywhere on the web, so converting solves sharing and embedding problems while keeping files small.

HEIC to WebP — FAQ

How does HEIC conversion work without uploading?

We load a HEIC decoder (heic2any) on demand the first time you convert one. It runs in your browser; nothing is sent to a server.

Why are my photos HEIC?

iPhones default to HEIC to save space. You can also change this in iOS camera settings.

Is WebP better than HEIC for the web?

For web use, yes — WebP is widely supported by browsers while HEIC often isn't.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes, it runs in mobile browsers too.
