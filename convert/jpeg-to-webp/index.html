JPEG to WebP — FAQ

What's the difference between JPEG and JPG?

None. They are the same format; .jpg was historically used on systems with 3-letter extension limits.

Is WebP better than JPEG?

For the web, generally yes — smaller files at comparable quality, plus optional transparency and animation.

Can I batch convert JPEGs?

Yes, drop as many as you like and download them together as a ZIP.
