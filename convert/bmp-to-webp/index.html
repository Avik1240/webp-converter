BMP to WebP — FAQ

Why are BMP files so large?

BMP is uncompressed — every pixel is stored raw. Converting to WebP applies efficient compression.

Will the image look different?

At high quality, no visible difference, but the file is dramatically smaller.

Can I convert many BMPs?

Yes, batch conversion and ZIP download are supported.
