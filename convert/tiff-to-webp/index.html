Why convert TIFF to WebP?

TIFF preserves maximum quality for archival and print, which is overkill for the web. WebP retains the visual quality your audience needs at a tiny fraction of the size.

TIFF to WebP — FAQ

How is TIFF supported in the browser?

We load a lightweight TIFF decoder (UTIF) on demand the first time you convert a TIFF. It runs locally; no upload occurs.

Does it handle multi-page TIFFs?

The first page is converted. Multi-page export is on the roadmap.

Why not keep TIFF?

TIFF isn't supported by web browsers for display and is far too large for web pages.

Is there a size limit?

Files up to 50MB by default; this is adjustable when self-hosting.
