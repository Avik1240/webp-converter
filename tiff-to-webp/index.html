TIFF to WebP

Convert TIFF to WebP Online

Convert browser-supported TIFF images to lighter WebP files on your device.

Browser support note: TIFF decoding is only built into Safari. In Chrome, Firefox, and Edge, TIFF files usually cannot be decoded and will show a conversion error. On those browsers, convert TIFF to PNG or JPG first.

Bring TIFF images to the web

TIFF is popular in print and archival workflows but is rarely ideal for a website. WebP can make compatible TIFF imagery easier to distribute online. TIFF decoding support differs by browser.

TIFF to WebP FAQ

Why will some TIFF files not open?

TIFF has many variants and most browsers do not decode all of them. This tool works with files the current browser can render.

Is the conversion private?

Yes. No image is sent to this site’s server.
