# Content Plan — 100 Articles

Editorial roadmap for organic growth. Articles are grouped into topic clusters that
interlink (each cluster links up to a pillar page and across to the converter tool).
Priority: **P1** = publish first (high intent / low competition), **P2** = second wave,
**P3** = long-tail fill.

Conventions for every article:
- One primary keyword + 2–4 secondary keywords.
- Target a Featured Snippet (definition, table, or numbered steps near the top).
- Include a "People Also Ask" FAQ block with FAQ schema.
- Internal links: ≥2 to relevant `/convert/*` pages + ≥2 to sibling articles.
- Clear CTA to the converter.

## Production cadence (suggested)

- **Weeks 1–4:** all P1 articles (~22) — fastest path to ranking on intent-rich terms.
- **Weeks 5–10:** P2 articles, interlinking back to P1 pillars.
- **Ongoing:** P3 long-tail, 2–3/week, capturing "People Also Ask" coverage.

## Workflow to scale this

Articles share one HTML shell. The cleanest next step is to extend `scripts/generate.mjs`
to template blog posts from a `scripts/data/posts.json` (front-matter style: title,
description, keyword, body sections, FAQ) so new articles are a data edit, not a hand-built
page — mirroring how the `/convert/*` pages already work.
