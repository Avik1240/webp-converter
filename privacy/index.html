Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

The short version

Your images are converted entirely inside your browser using the Canvas API. They are never uploaded to, stored on, or processed by our servers. We cannot see your files.

Image processing

All conversion happens locally on your device. When you close or reload the page, any images you added are discarded from memory. We do not receive, transmit, log, or retain any file you convert.

Analytics

If analytics is enabled on this site, we use Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate usage (for example, page views and which converters are used). Analytics data is collected with IP anonymization. We track anonymous events such as "images uploaded" or "conversion completed" — these events contain counts and file types only, never file contents or file names. You can block analytics with any standard content blocker without affecting the converter.

Advertising

If advertising (such as Google AdSense) is enabled on this site, the ad provider may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads. See Google's advertising policies for details. Ads never interact with, access, or affect the images you convert.

Cookies and local storage

The converter itself does not set cookies and does not use local storage. Any cookies present originate from the optional analytics or advertising services described above.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data. We have no user accounts and no database of users.

Your rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data ourselves, there is typically nothing for us to delete or export. For data held by Google (analytics or ads), consult Google's privacy controls. If you have questions about this policy, open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the project evolves. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
