A modern format for JPEG photos

JPEG and JPG are the same common photo format. Re-encoding photos as WebP can improve transfer size, which is helpful for portfolios, product images, and content-heavy sites.

JPEG to WebP FAQ

Are JPEG and JPG different?

No. They describe the same image format; the different extension came from older operating-system conventions.

Yes. Add multiple images and choose Convert all to process the queue.
