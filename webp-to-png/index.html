PNG is useful when a platform needs a broadly compatible lossless image or when you need to retain transparency. PNG files are often larger, so use it when compatibility matters more than file size.

WebP to PNG FAQ

Will transparent areas remain transparent?

Yes. The converter preserves transparency when exporting WebP images as PNG.

Why is the PNG larger than the WebP?

PNG commonly uses lossless compression, which produces larger files than lossy WebP for photographic imagery.
