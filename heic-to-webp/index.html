Convert HEIC to WebP Online

Export compatible HEIC photos as web-ready WebP files directly in your browser.

Browser support note: HEIC decoding is only built into Safari (macOS and iOS). In Chrome, Firefox, and Edge, HEIC files usually cannot be decoded and will show a conversion error. On those browsers, export your iPhone photos as JPG first, or open this page in Safari.

From iPhone photos to WebP

HEIC is common on modern Apple devices, while WebP is widely supported on the web. Browser support for HEIC varies, so this tool works only when your current browser can decode the selected file.

HEIC to WebP FAQ

Why can’t my browser read HEIC?

HEIC decoding is not built into every browser. If it fails, convert through your device’s photo export or use a dedicated local tool.

Does this upload my iPhone photo?

No. The image stays on your device throughout processing.
